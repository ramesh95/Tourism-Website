The natives of Odisha (Formerly Orissa) welcome you to experience the multitude wonders of this miraculous state.
Positioned on the east coast, Odisha (Formerly Orissa), also known as The Soul of India, is a spiritual land where the hoary history and the modern today form a harmonious blend - where ancient temples mingle with contemporary five star hotels. A land filled with awe inspiring monuments, thousands of master craftsmen and artists, numerous wildlife sanctuaries, stunning natural landscapes...

Odisha has always been a traveller's paradise.

Ama Tourism was set up on 30th March, 1982 to look after the viable activities of the Government in the service industry of tourism in the state. Ama Tourism has come a long way and completed 25 years of flourishing business in tourism sector and is one of the booming Company in the service industry in the State of Odisha.

The company is governed by the Board of Directors allotted by the Government. The Board of Directors consists of 10 members. The shareholders consist of 6 members, all of whom are Government nominees. All the shares are held by the Governor of Odisha, except 6 shares which are held by its nominees. The Managing Director is the Executive Head of Ama Tourism.

“Ama Tourism” believes that promotion of the State itself as a leading tourist destination, is a vindication of its Mission Statement that reads "At Ama Tourism we strive to endow with the best Tourism-related services to our guests. We guarantee never to forfeit our professional integrity and to generate the utmost quality effort feasible and vow to stay true to it. It is our dedication to create a enduring bond with our guests and provide them with great value in everything we offer".

For nearly three decades now Ama Tourism has been at the wheel of the tourism industry in Odisha. We have quite accurately been the helmsman on board inviting and guiding tourists from the nation and from overseas on a charming and enriching journey across the beautiful land of Odisha.

Set up as a Public Limited Company with a Memorandum and Articles of Association with a fullfledged Board of Directors, we have been both munificent host and lead to millions of visitors from India and across the world over the years. Odisha Tourism plays a dynamic part in all viable aspects of tourism, including providing accommodation, organizing sightseeing tours, and other attractive packages.
