 Olive Ridely Tours

Destination Covered :  Odisha.

Tour Duration :  05 Nights / 06 Days.

Day 01 :Arrival at Bhubaneswar
Sightseeing of tribal museum ( closed on Monday ), Khandagiri & Udaigiri Jain caves ( oldest rock cut caves ). Overnight at Bhubaneswar.

Day 02 :Bhubaneswar - Konark - Puri
Morning after breakfast drive to Puri enroute visit Dhauli ( Budhist stupa / peace pagoda ), Pipli ( the applique work village ) and Konark Sun Temple ( famously known as black pagoda ). Then proceed to Puri and check in to the hotel. Evening visit of Lord Jagannath Temple (Non Hindus are not allowed) / Relax on the golden beach of puri.



Day 03 : Puri - Chilika - Gopalpur
Morning drive to Chilika lake at Barkul ( the largest Ramsar site of Asia and famous for its migratory birds coming from distance places ). Take the boat cruise to visit the birds island . Then proceed to Goplapur nad check in to the hotel. Overnight at Gopalpur.

Day 04 : Gopalpur - Jaugad - Gopalpur + Olive Ridley
Morning after breakfast visit to Pati Sonpur beach. Return back to hotel. Later in the evening transfer by car to the Turtles sightseeing at the Rushik-ulya river with packed dinner. Come back to the hotel at night and retire for the day. Overnight at Gopalpur.

Day 05 : Gopalpur - Bhubaneswar
Morning after breakfast drive back to Bhubaneswar and check in to the hotel. Later half day sightseeing of Lingaraj, Parsurameswar, Mukteswar ( Mukteswar temple, one of the most prominent temples of Bhubaneswar, has been constructed in the style that is quite similar to the one used in the Kalinga School of Temple Architecture and know as the 'gem of Orissan architecture' ), Rajarani temple. Overnight at Bhubaneswar.

Day 06 : Gopalpur - Taptapani
Morning check out from the hotel and transfer to airport for onwards journey.
